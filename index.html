I am Yujia Zhang (Ph.D.), a Machine Learning Resident at Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii) in Edmonton, Canada.

I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the University of Alberta (Sept. 2019 – Nov. 2024). My research and applied work focus on knowledge graphs, language models, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and agentic LLM applications.

Summary
Machine learning researcher with 8+ years of experience developing and deploying ML models, specializing in knowledge graphs and language models. Experienced with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and cloud platforms (GCP).

Research

  • Knowledge Graphs: topic hierarchies, hierarchical analysis, and generative modeling for KGs.
  • LLMs for Information Extraction: fine-tuning and data augmentation for triple extraction from text.
  • RAG & Agentic LLMs: building practical, industry-ready systems and deployment pipelines.
